The 4-Week Breakdown

Each week builds on the last. By the end, you'll move, hit, and think like a fighter.

01

Foundation

Build your stance, footwork, and the fundamentals that everything else is built on. Learn to move right before you learn to hit hard.

02

Combinations

Start putting punches together. Jab-cross, hooks, uppercuts — real combinations that work in the ring, not just on a bag.

03

Defense & Movement

Slips, rolls, angles, and ring IQ. Learn to not get hit — the part most programs skip entirely.

04

Putting It All Together

Full rounds. Offense, defense, conditioning — everything from weeks 1-3 combined into fight-simulation sessions.
