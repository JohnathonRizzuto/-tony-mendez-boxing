// 06 — Questions

Things People Always Ask.

Is this for beginners?
Yes. The program starts at zero — stance, hand wraps, basic punches — and builds up week by week. Whether you've never thrown a punch or you've been training for years, you'll get something out of it.
How does the program work?
Once you enroll, you get instant access to all 4 weeks of video lessons. Train at your own pace. Most students go through it once over a month, then loop back to drill specific sections.
Do I need equipment?
Hand wraps and gloves are recommended — basic gear, not expensive. A heavy bag is ideal, but a pillow, a held pad, or a sparring partner all work. You can also get a lot out of shadowboxing alone if that's all you've got. The point is to put the rounds in, not to buy a gym.
Is this good for kids and teens?
Yes — the fundamentals are the same at every age. For younger kids, a parent or guardian should supervise the contact and sparring sections.
How long do I have access?
Lifetime. Pay once, train forever. You also get free updates whenever new material is added to the program.
